what is the difference between osci-render and sosci?

osci-render is an oscilloscope music synthesizer. It is software designed to convert images and 3D animations into visuals to display on a real or software oscilloscope. It can generate audio from 3D objects, text, images, and more, allowing for creative audio-visual music-making.

the premium version includes:

  • the same high-quality built-in visualiser that sosci has
  • the ability to record visuals
  • support for MP4 and MOV video formats
  • syphon/spout input
  • improved text rendering

Note: sosci is not included with osci-render premium.

sosci is an analogue oscilloscope simulator. It is a software oscilloscope that you run on your computer instead of buying a physical one.

sosci saves you $$$ and effort by no longer needing to purchase and maintain an analogue oscilloscope + audio interface.

it is purely an audio visualiser and does not generate sound of its own, instead it is used to visualise audio from other sources.


Note: sosci is not included with osci-render premium.

  • if you want a plugin just for visualising audio, get sosci!
  • if you want a fully-fledged video + audio synthesiser that converts 3D objects, text, video, and images into audio, get osci-render!
sosci is focused on being a visualiser, while osci-render is a synthesiser with many more features. both can visualise audio, but osci-render premium includes the beautiful visualiser and recording capabilities from sosci, plus much more.

Note: sosci is not included with osci-render premium.
