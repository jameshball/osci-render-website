an audio plugin for simulating realistic analogue oscilloscopes

sosci is a super-realistic software oscilloscope for simulating the look and feel of a real analogue oscilloscope from the 20th century.


features

 • audio upsampling with realistic artifacts

 • video sharing with Syphon/Spout

 • recording to .mp4

 • presets for real oscilloscopes


purchase as an audio plugin, or standalone app, on Windows, macOS, and Linux* below.
