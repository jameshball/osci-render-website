an audio plugin for simulating realistic analogue oscilloscopes


sosci is a super-realistic software oscilloscope that simulates the look and feel of a real analogue oscilloscope from the 20th century.

Audio Upsampling

Realistic artifacts and authentic analogue oscilloscope behavior

3 Channel Brightness Input

Z-axis control for variable brightness and intensity effects

Video Sharing

Real-time output with Syphon/Spout support for live visuals

Recording

Export your oscilloscope visuals as MP4 files

Real Oscilloscope Presets

Authentic presets based on vintage analogue oscilloscopes

Multiple Formats

Available as VST, AU, and standalone application

based on the amazing XXY Oscilloscope by Neil Thapen.
